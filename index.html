How to Play
  • ↺
    Rotate Left / Right — Spin 90° to change direction
  • ⬆
    Move — Advance 1 tile (walls & bots block)
  • 📦
    Load — Load a paintball into the gun
  • 🎯
    Shoot — Fire! Paintball paints tiles to the wall
  • 🔄
    Repeat / While / If — Control flow blocks
  • 💥
    Burst — Load 4× without shooting and your bot explodes a diamond of paint around itself!
  • 🏆
    Most tiles painted in your color at the end wins!
